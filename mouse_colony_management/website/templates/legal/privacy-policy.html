Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide to us while using the Mouse Colony Management System.

Data Collection

We collect personal information such as names, email addresses, and user credentials, as well as research data related to the management of the mouse colony.

Data Usage

Collected data is used to maintain system functionality, track colony health, and ensure compliance with research protocols. Your data will not be used for any commercial purposes.

Security

We take all necessary measures to ensure the security of your data, including encryption and secure access protocols.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, you can reach us at itservices@abdn.ac.uk.
